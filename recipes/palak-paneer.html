Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 250-300g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 bunches of fresh spinach (palak)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed or chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1-2 tbsp cream (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)

Steps to Follow

  1. Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water for 2–3 minutes, then transfer to ice water.
  2. Blend the spinach into a smooth puree and keep aside.
  3. In a pan, heat oil or ghee and sauté cumin seeds until they splutter.
  4. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  5. Mix in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; sauté for a minute.
  6. Add chopped or pureed tomatoes, turmeric, and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  7. Add the spinach puree and stir well. Cook for 5–7 minutes.
  8. Add paneer cubes and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  9. Optional: Add cream for a richer texture. Adjust salt.
  10. Serve hot with roti or rice.
