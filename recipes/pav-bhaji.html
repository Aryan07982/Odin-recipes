Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

  • 2-3 potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 cup cauliflower (boiled & mashed)
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 capsicum (finely chopped)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp Pav Bhaji masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter (2-3 tbsp or more)
  • Water (as needed)
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)
  • Lemon (for serving)
  • Pav buns (store-bought)
  • Butter (to toast pav)

Steps to Follow

  1. Boil potatoes, cauliflower, and green peas until soft. Mash them well.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter and sauté chopped onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and capsicum; cook for a few minutes.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  5. Add pav bhaji masala, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the mashed veggies and mix everything thoroughly.
  7. Adjust consistency by adding water as needed. Simmer for 10–15 minutes, stirring often.
  8. Mash again while cooking for a smoother texture. Add more butter if desired.
  9. Toast pav buns with butter on a tawa until golden.
  10. Serve the bhaji hot with buttered pav, chopped onions, coriander, and a lemon wedge.
