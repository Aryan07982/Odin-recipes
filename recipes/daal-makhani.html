Ingredients

  • 1 cup Urad daal
  • 1/4 cup Rajma
  • 1 large finely chopped Onion
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • +2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Water (as needed)

Steps to Follow

  1. Soak black lentils and kidney beans overnight in water.
  2. Pressure cook them with salt until soft (usually 6–8 whistles).
  3. In a pan, heat butter and sauté chopped onions until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook until fragrant.
  5. Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala. Cook until oil separates.
  6. Add the cooked dal and beans to the masala mixture. Mix well.
  7. Simmer on low heat for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add water if too thick.
  8. Add cream and simmer for another 5–10 minutes for richness.
  9. Garnish with coriander and an extra blob of butter before serving.
